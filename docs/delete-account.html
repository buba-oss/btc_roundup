Request Account Deletion

To delete your BTC Round Up account and associated data:

Option 1: In-App (Fastest)

Open BTC Round Up → Settings → Account → Delete Account

Option 2: Email Request

Send email to: privacy@btcroundup.app

Subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Include: Your registered email address

Data Deleted

  • Account profile (name, email)
  • Bank connection data
  • Bitcoin wallet addresses
  • Transaction history
  • App usage data

Data Retained (Legal Requirements)

  • Tax records: 7 years (financial regulations)
  • Transaction logs: 5 years (anti-fraud)
  • These are anonymized and not linked to your identity

Processing time: Up to 30 days

Questions: privacy@btcroundup.app
