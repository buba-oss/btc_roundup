Privacy Policy for BTC Round Up

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to operate the BTC Round Up app and provide its features:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials
  • User-Provided Data: Spending amounts entered by the user for round-up calculations, Bitcoin wallet addresses (optional)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version (for security and troubleshooting)
  • Usage Data: App interaction data and crash reports (via Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate voluntary round-up savings amounts
  • To display savings history and statistics
  • To maintain account security and prevent abuse
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

BTC Round Up does not provide financial or investment advice. All actions are initiated and approved by the user.

3. Data Sharing

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data either in-app or by email.

We share data only with:

  • Infrastructure Providers: Google Firebase for authentication, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Third-Party Services Chosen by the User: Cryptocurrency platforms accessed independently by the user outside the app

BTC Round Up does not access bank accounts, does not store private keys, and does not custody funds.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted transmission (TLS) and secure authentication. We do not store full bank account numbers or private keys.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us immediately at privacy@btcroundup.app to request deletion.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request account and data deletion
  • Export your data where applicable

7. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your BTC Round Up account and associated data using one of the following methods:

Option 1: In-App (Fastest)

Open BTC Round Up → Settings → Account → Delete Account

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to: privacy@btcroundup.app
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Include your registered email address.

Data Deleted

  • Account profile (email)
  • User-provided wallet addresses
  • Usage and app interaction data

Data Retained (Legal Requirements)

  • Minimal transaction logs retained for anti-fraud and legal compliance
  • All retained data is anonymized and not linked to your identity

Processing time: up to 30 days.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests:
Email: privacy@btcroundup.app
